Getting to Berkeley

The Bay Area's three major airports, San Francisco (SFO), Oakland (OAK), and San Jose (SJC), are all within driving distance of Berkeley. Most international flights arrive at SFO. We recommend OAK and SFO for their proximity to Berkeley.

Getting to the Brazilian Room

The Brazilian Room is located in the hills of Berkeley inside Tilden Regional Park. While it is technically reachable by public transportation, we advise guests to carpool to reach the venue. Directions from common locations are available on the East Bay Regional Parks website, but when in doubt, use Google Maps.
